About Me

Zhiming Chen (born 1994) is a comic creator and young scholar, known for contributions to the fields of film, media culture, and comic. Chen has a notable background in media culture research, with significant works in comic projects, research publications, and media analysis.

Early Life and Education

Zhiming Chen was born in Jixi, China in 1994. Career

Comic Projects

  • "Girl's Fantasy Romance Special: Unknown Flower Blooms with Love": A short webcomic created in collaboration with WEBTOON, which explores the romantic thoughts of a young girl.
  • "Fall in Love with a Big Sly": This comic tells the story of a kind-hearted protagonist who, after accidentally traveling back in time to ancient China, becomes a painter in the imperial palace and gets entangled in royal power struggles.
  • "Analysis of Anime Works: A Multimodal Perspective": Co-edited by Chen, this forthcoming book is a comprehensive guide for media professionals, examining the interplay between anime content and media technology. The book is set to be published in 2025.

Interests

Chen's interests include academic research in cultural studies and media history. She is particularly passionate about anime and manga studies, which is reflected in her professional and creative endeavors. Outside of her academic pursuits, Chen enjoys exploring different cultures through travel, often finding inspiration for her research through these experiences.

Personal Life

Chen resides in Fuzhou, China, where she continues to pursue her interests in comics. She is dedicated to advancing her knowledge and skills, contributing to the fields of film, animation, game, and comic.
